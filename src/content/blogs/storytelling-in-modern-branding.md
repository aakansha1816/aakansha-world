Think of narrative frameworks as the scaffolding around which a brand's entire communication strategy is built. At the foundation sits the **origin story** — why the brand exists beyond making money. Layered on top are the **character archetypes** that give the brand a recognisable personality.

### The Three Pillars

1. **Authenticity** — Consumers can sense fabricated narratives. Grounding stories in real experiences builds trust.
2. **Consistency** — A story told differently across channels creates cognitive dissonance. Unified narrative threads matter.
3. **Evolution** — Static stories stagnate. The best brands let their narratives grow with cultural shifts.
