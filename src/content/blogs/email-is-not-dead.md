## The Oldest Channel Still Wins

Every few years, someone declares email dead. And every year, email marketing delivers an average return of $36 for every $1 spent. No social platform, no paid channel, no emerging technology comes close to that efficiency.

The problem isn't the channel — it's the execution. Most brand emails are forgettable: generic subject lines, wall-of-text layouts, and offers that feel like they were written by a spreadsheet.

### The Subject Line Test

Your subject line competes with messages from friends, family, and colleagues. It needs to earn the open. Rules of thumb:

- **Curiosity over clarity** — "The metric we stopped tracking" beats "Q3 Marketing Report"
- **Specificity** — "5 slides that closed a $2M deal" outperforms "Sales tips inside"
- **Personality** — Write like a human, not an institution

### The Segmentation Ladder

2. **Behavioural**: Clicked on product X, opened last 3 emails, abandoned cart
3. **Predictive**: Likely to churn, high-value potential, ready to upgrade
