## The Brief

Pulse, a fitness-tech startup, was preparing to launch their second-generation wearable. They had strong product reviews but virtually no social presence. The goal: build a community from scratch and generate buzz ahead of launch day.

Teaser content featuring close-up product shots with cryptic copy. No product name, just intrigue. We seeded these across Instagram, TikTok, and X to build curiosity.

Creator partnerships with 12 mid-tier fitness and wellness influencers. Each creator shared their personal "pulse" — what motivates them — while naturally integrating the product into their routines. No scripts, full creative freedom.
