## The Brief

Groundwork, an environmental non-profit focused on urban green spaces, needed to raise $250,000 during their year-end campaign — their most ambitious target yet. Previous campaigns had plateaued, and they needed a fresh creative approach that would energise existing donors and attract new ones.

- **Champions** (top 5% of donors) — motivated by impact reports and personal recognition
- **Regulars** (monthly donors) — motivated by community belonging and progress updates
- **Lapsed** (donated once, never returned) — needed a compelling reason to re-engage

Each group received a tailored campaign experience.

## Channel Strategy

### Email (Primary Driver)

A five-email sequence over three weeks, personalised by donor segment:

1. **Story email** — a first-person account from a community member whose neighbourhood was transformed
2. **Impact email** — data visualisation showing exactly where money goes
3. **Milestone email** — triggered when the garden reached 25%, 50%, 75%
4. **Social proof email** — featuring donor testimonials and count
5. **Urgency email** — 48-hour countdown with match offer

## Results

- **$352,000** raised — **40% above target**
- **1,400** new first-time donors acquired
- **28%** reactivation rate among lapsed donors
- Email campaign achieved **52% open rate** and **8.4% click-through**
- The digital garden was visited **23,000 times** during the campaign
